Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 16, 2025

Introduction

To-Do Master ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Data Collection

To-Do Master is designed with privacy in mind. All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Information Stored Locally

The following information is stored locally on your device:

  • Your tasks and to-do items
  • Scheduled reminders and their settings
  • Voice recordings you create
  • App preferences and settings
  • Progress tracking data

Permissions

To-Do Master may request the following permissions:

  • Microphone: Required for voice note recording feature
  • Notifications: Required to send you reminders for your scheduled tasks
  • Storage: Required to save your tasks and voice recordings locally

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information depends on your device's security measures. We recommend keeping your device secure with a password or biometric lock.

Third-Party Services

To-Do Master does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your data remains private and on your device.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since all data is stored locally, parents have full control over their children's use of the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
